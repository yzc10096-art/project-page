Abstract

We propose MGFDK, a multi-modal gait analysis framework for depression screening. Consecutive frames are stacked to form temporal gait templates for each modality (RGB/IR/Depth), processed by modality-specific ResNet-50 encoders and fused before a Transformer-based head. A consistency loss bridges tri-modal and bi-modal settings to handle missing modalities.

Highlights
  • Robust to missing modalities
  • ResNet-50 encoders + Transformer head
  • Simple MSE guidance across modes
